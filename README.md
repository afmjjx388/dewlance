## What Is Dewlance, Exactly?

Dewlance is a web hosting provider that's been running for over 15 years. They offer shared hosting, reseller hosting, and Linux VPS — all running on NVMe SSDs and AMD EPYC processors, which is genuinely impressive for the price range they're playing in.

Their servers are spread across three locations for shared and reseller hosting: **United States, United Kingdom, and Australia**. VPS is US-based.

They keep the team small and lean — apparently about 6 people — which cuts costs and lets them offer pricing that makes larger hosts look embarrassing. The flip side? You're not getting enterprise-grade polish. But for the use cases they're targeting (individuals, small businesses, resellers on a budget), the trade-off is pretty reasonable.

## What to Keep in Mind

They're a small operation. You're not getting the hand-holding experience of a big managed host. DirectAdmin instead of cPanel might require a small learning curve if you're used to cPanel. VPS is US-only. Some third-party reviewers have noted occasional inconsistency in support response times — likely a staffing and time-zone factor given the India-based team.
